Unizik Tech Community Branding Case Study

The Unizik Tech Community help students develop, grow and hone their skills in tech that they would not otherwise find time to do. Our job is to improve recognition for the community to get more people involved.

Memorable, digital and timeless.

We created a logo that stands out and aligns with the brand’s mission. We decided to take a less sophisticated approach to make the logo more memorable and timeless.

The Logo is the community name initials, with “T” in whitespace, created with a square as it’s building block which makes the logo look digital to represent the community mission into tech.
